About Igatpuri

The Heaven of Maharashtra

 

Often called, “The Heaven of Maharashtra” Igatpuri is a hill station at an elevation of 600m above sea level, making it a very popular choice for weekend getaways for most of the year. Located in the tranquil environs of Igatpuri, is easily accessible from Mumbai 130 km, 2 hours approx, Pune 240 km, 4 hours approx & just 43 kms from Nashik.
