AMK Hills Igatpuri

Luxurious Weekend Homes & Lifestyle Villas for Holiday Home Concept at Bhavali Hills, Igatpuri. Igatpuri is a hill station situated at the height of 600 Mts. from sea level in the evergreen territory of Western Ghat. Igatpuri is a tiny town situated in between Mumbai – Nashik express highway.

Surrounded by Natural Attractions like Mountains & Valleys, Rivers & Lakes, Waterfalls, Wildlife Sanctuary and much more Ideal destination for Trekking, Jungle Trails, Zipline, Boating & River Rafting, Rappelling, Mountain Biking and many more fun activities. Visit the Dhamma Giri Vipassana Academy, Ghatandevi & Saptashrungi Devi Temples, Bhavli & Vaitarna Dams, and many Forts all close by.

Igatpuri is a hill station at an elevation of 600m above sea level, making it a very popular choice for weekend getaways for most of the year. Located in the tranquil environs of Igatpuri, is easily accessible from Mumbai 130 km, 2 hours approx, Pune 240 km, 4 hours approx & just 43 kms from Nashik.
